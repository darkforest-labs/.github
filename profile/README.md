## The Core Idea

We build AI that runs on physics, not software.

Traditional AI requires massive digital infrastructure ‚Äî GPUs, data centers, gigawatts of power. We're taking a different approach: **analog neural networks** that perform inference directly in the physical substrate, with no clock, no ADC, and no Von Neumann bottleneck.

The result? AI systems that operate at microwatt scales while remaining invisible in the noise floor.

## Why Analog?

Digital AI hits fundamental limits:

- **Power wall** ‚Äî GPUs burn kilowatts for inference
- **Memory wall** ‚Äî data movement costs more than computation
- **Latency wall** ‚Äî clock cycles add up

Analog AI sidesteps all three. Our networks compute in continuous time, where "memory" and "processing" are the same physical phenomenon.
